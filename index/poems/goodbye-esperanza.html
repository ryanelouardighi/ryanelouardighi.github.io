Goodbye Esperanza


The end draws nearer and nearer. But I am not ready just yet. I still barely know her, her family, her history. Not yet. I do not want to leave, leave someone else behind, someone with the same concerns as me. But it happens nonetheless.

110: She leaves. And I must wave goodbye and give her good tidings. Only the second friend I have ever had, and she must leave.

Goodbye Esperanza, goodbye, I say. And the tempest is unleashed.



Ad astra per aspera.
