“I Wish for Death”,

2025 April 22, 16:49 (pr. April 24, 13:05)

Take me while I am writing,

Take me while I am sleeping.

Take me without chiding,

Take me without peeping.


You see that I am eager

for you to be prudent;

act as an old teacher,

and I will be your lone student.


The day of my death shall be joyous.

I shall finally be free;

no longer an annoyance

for people to judge or see.


So take me quick

without making me wait;

take me when you wish,

but preferably soon and not late.
