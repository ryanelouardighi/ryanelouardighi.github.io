“Document 018 ("Deliverances")”,

2025 May 5, 17:51

What shall deliver me to my death?


What shall transfer me between realms?


Shall it be the
CRACK!
of the gun,

or the sharp and quick slice of the knife?


Will the railroad tracks make a mess of me,

or might the I-95 send me to rest in peace?


Will I end up doing the act myself,

Or continue to write about it pointlessly?


Oh, I have long-awaited my own deliverance,

to which I will owe a debt for removing my strife!
