“Le méchant”,

2025 February 27, 18:07

I am a creature.

Shrouded in vanity,

Engulfed in self-pity,

Shrinking and becoming weaker.


I am alone.

Obstinate and standing apart

For my wicked, incorrigible heart

Slowly corrupts my extroverted soul.


I am fearful.

Those who stand with me

always drift and leave

as I remain and grow tearful.


“You are your biggest cheerleader”;

A pity that is, coming from my one true rival.
