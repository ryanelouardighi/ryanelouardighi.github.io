“The Divine Feminine”,

2025 May 6, 07:05

She is a woman!


The softness of her hands

proves such.


She is a woman!


Look at her beauty;

Listen to her softness in speech;

Long for her.


She is a woman!


Reserved and kept away is her flower;

and with it, she holds power.

Her obedience and acquiescence

speaks volumes of her divine femininity.


She is a “woman”!
