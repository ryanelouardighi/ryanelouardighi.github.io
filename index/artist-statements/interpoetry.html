Interpoetry

Interpoetry, my ninth chapbook, was written on 2026 January 12; it is 5 pages long, excluding the front cover. I don’t really remember much about why I wrote it. I do know that it was written during 23:32 (“Interpoem #1”) to 23:53 (“Interpoem #5 (____)”), so I can only assume it was one of my late night ideas. I also know that I imagined the reader filling in the blanks like a Mad Libs book to bend the structure of the material in Interpoetry to their will.
