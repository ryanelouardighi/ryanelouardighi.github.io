Les perdues

Les perdues, my fifteenth chapbook, was arranged on 2026 February 23 at 15:41; it is 15 pages long, excluding the front cover. It is unique as it is a return to curating instead of purely constructing books, in the style that Humble Beginnings and ;, my first and second chapbook respectively, were curated from pre-existing material.

I arranged Les perdues, and its thicker cousin Les perdues toujours trouveront leur maison, because I wanted to have a chapbook that was exclusively composed of my French poetry.

Originally there was only one volume of my French poetry, also called Les perdues; however, I decided to split it off into two volumes, with Les perdues being the new volume cut down to what I found to be the best poetry, and the old volume, renamed Les perdues toujours trouveront leur maison, being the unabridged volume with all of my French poetry up to the point it was arranged.
